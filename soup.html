Spiced Carrot and Lentil Soup


This soup is one of our most popular recipes, boasting a delicious blend of spice, flavour and also low calorie. This is one you don't want to miss!

This dish will take less than 30 minutes and serves 4!


Ingredients

  • 2tbsp cumin seeds
  • A pinch of chilli flakes
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 600g carrots washed and grated
  • 140g split red lentils
  • 1 litre hot veegtable stock (cube is fine)
  • 125ml of milk
  • plain yogurt and naan bread to servce

Lets get cooking!

  1. Heat a large saucepan and dry-fry the cumin seeds and chilli flakes for 1 minute
  2. Scoop out half from the pan and set aside
  3. Add the olive oil, carrots, lentils and stock and the milk and bring to boil
  4. Simmer for 15 minutes until the lentils are swollen and softened
  5. Put the soup through a blender or processor to smoothen or leave chunky if you prefer
  6. Season to taste, add a dollop of yogurt and serve with a warm naan bread
