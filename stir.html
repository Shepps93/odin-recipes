Super Fast Stir-Fry!


This first recipe is for an incredibly fast and healthy Stir-Fry which encourages a great lifestyle whilst still tasting damn good. And it's dead simple


Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 red chilli sliced
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 500g mixed vegetables of your choice
  • 1.5 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp sweet chilli sauce
