Super Fast Stir-Fry!


This first recipe is for an incredibly fast and healthy vegetable Stir-Fry which encourages a great lifestyle whilst still tasting damn good. Feel free to grill some meat or fish on the side to add in some extra protein to the dish.

Total preparation and cooking time is only 10 minutes!


Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 red chilli sliced
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 500g mixed vegetables of your choice
  • 1.5 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp sweet chilli sauce

Lets get cooking!

  1. Heat the oil in a large frying pan or wok
  2. Add the chilli and gralic and fry for one minute
  3. Add the vegetables and fold and fry for 2-3 minutes
  4. Now add the soy and chilli sauce and mix well
  5. Cook for a further 2-3 minutes until the veg is tender and you're done!
