Tamarind Prawn Curry


This recipe focuses on creating a beautifully flavoured Prawn curry which will be a hit with your family and friends while also being low calorie and guilt-free.

This recipe will only take around 20 minutes from scratch and serves 2!


Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 1 finely chopped red chilli
  • 1 tbsp grated ginger
  • 1 tsp tumeric
  • 1tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 400g canned cherry tomatos
  • 1-2tbsp tamarind paste
  • 250g raw king prawns
  • 250g cooked basmati rice
  • handful of coriander leaves to serve

Lets get cooking!

  1. Heat the oil in the frying pan and cook the onions for 5-8 minutes until golden
  2. Stir in the chilli, garlic and ginger and fry for another minute and then add the spices
  3. Add the cherry tomatoes and add some water filled with the tomato can
  4. Simmer for 5 minutes until the sauce thickens
  5. Add the tamarind and ptawns and continuet simmer for a further 2-3 minutes
  6. Finally, serve the curry on top of the rice and scatter the coriandr on top!
